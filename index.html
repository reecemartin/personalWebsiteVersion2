Photo Tagging and Management App

This Cross-Platform Desktop application allows for mass photo management and sorting. Users instantly have access to all the photos in their file system and can sort them through tagging. the integration of an Open Source Tensorflow Photo-Classifier allows the application to intelligently suggest tags for the photos based on their content.
